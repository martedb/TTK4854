About GhostNetBusters

Why We Built This

GhostNetBusters is a student-driven project created to raise awareness about ghost nets ‚Äî abandoned fishing gear that continues to trap and kill marine life long after it's been lost. These nets are a huge problem in our oceans, and we wanted to do something practical to highlight the issue.

As part of our work, we developed an interactive map that helps visualize ghost net locations along the coast and prioritize clean-up efforts based on ecological importance. The tool highlights areas like kelp forests, protected marine zones, spawning grounds, and regions with red-listed species.
