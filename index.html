About GhostNetBusters

Abandoned fishing gear, known as ghost nets, threaten marine ecosystems by entangling wildlife and damaging habitats.

We focus on kelp forests, protected areas, spawning grounds, and red-listed species to prioritize net removal.

Join us in the fight against ghost nets and contribute to the protection of vital marine ecosystems. 🌊🐚
