Our interactive map allows you to explore the locations of ghost nets across various marine regions.

By using this map, you can better understand the spread of ghost nets and contribute to focused, data-driven efforts.

Spawning Areas

Spawning areas are where fish lay their eggs and hatch. These zones are crucial for reproduction and early development. Nets in these areas pose a significant risk to vulnerable individuals, and decomposing fish can attract more entanglement.

Kelp forests

Kelp forests are especially productive ecosystems with a large diversity of species. However, they are shrinking due to pollution and other stressors. These areas tend to attract ghost nets, worsening the impact.
